Membership Benefits

The National Social Studies Association is a community of teachers, trainees, consultants and academics united by a common goal:
promoting excellence in social studies education. By actively supporting continuous professional development,
by harnessing the power of our community and by speaking with one voice on the issues that matter, we empower all social studies educators
to deliver the best possible outcomes in their educational setting.
